### Functionalities and Combinations

#### **Automation Controls**
- **`SID_AUTOMATION_ON`**:
  - Toggle visibility of "Session" or "Arranger" view.
  - **Shift + SID_AUTOMATION_ON**: Focus "Session" or "Arranger" view.

- **`SID_AUTOMATION_RECORD`**:
  - Toggle between "Detail/Clip" and "Detail/DeviceChain" subviews.
  - **Shift + SID_AUTOMATION_RECORD**: Focus "Detail/Clip" or "Detail/DeviceChain" view.

- **`SID_AUTOMATION_SNAPSHOT`**:
  - Toggle visibility of "Browser".
  - **Shift + SID_AUTOMATION_SNAPSHOT**: Focus "Browser".

- **`SID_AUTOMATION_TOUCH`**:
  - Toggle visibility of "Detail" view.
  - **Shift + SID_AUTOMATION_TOUCH**: Focus "Detail" view.

#### **Function Keys**
- **`SID_FUNC_UNDO`**: Perform undo operation.
- **`SID_FUNC_REDO`**: Perform redo operation.
- **`SID_FUNC_CANCEL`**: Toggle "Back to Arranger" mode.
- **`SID_FUNC_ENTER`**: Toggle "Draw Mode".
- **`SID_FUNC_MARKER`**: Set or delete a cue point.
- **`SID_FUNC_MIXER`**: Toggle "Follow Song" mode.

#### **Modifier Keys**
- **`SID_MOD_SHIFT`**: Activate "Shift" mode.
- **`SID_MOD_OPTION`**: Activate "Option" mode.
- **`SID_MOD_CTRL`**: Activate "Control" mode.
- **`SID_MOD_ALT`**: Activate "Alt" mode.
